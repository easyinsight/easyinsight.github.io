How can I improve my sales performance?

Easy Insight gives you the reporting power you need to maximize your sales performance. Keep track of what's in your pipeline so that you don't lose track of crucial deals. Keep track of vital metrics such as average deal size, time to close, and win rates.

Prebuilt Reports to Get You Started

As soon as you connect your Easy Insight account, you'll have dashboards and reports providing views into your sales data, your leads, your cases, and your tasks. You can export these reports into PDF and Excel, as well as setting them for recurring email delivery.

Extend Your Highrise Reporting With Custom Fields

Need a field like Expected Close or Probability that Highrise doesn't have by default? Easy Insight provides mechanisms to support adding and reporting on these fields. You can even combine your Highrise data with other systems such as Basecamp and FreshBooks.

Why Use Basecamp with Easy Insight?

Want to take your project management to the next level? You can get started with Easy Insight in a couple of clicks! If you don't already have an account, you can get started with a 30 day free trial with no obligation. The full power of enterprise business intelligence is yours for the taking, all delivered with incredible ease of use.

Visualize Your Project Management

Basecamp allows you to manage your project through a simple, clean interface. With Easy Insight, you can now take the information stored in Basecamp and create meaningful reports on it. How many tasks are completed on a weekly basis? What is the velocity of new tasks vs. tasks completed? Are you on track to completing all the tasks for a milestone? Visualizations in Easy Insight will make answers to questions such as these clear.

View Your Billing Status

When your projects involve consulting, it helps to see how much money is being billed. Combining the Basecamp data source with a spreadsheet of billing rates for a project will let you quickly see how much money has been billed over a period of a project, showing if the project is on budget or not. You can view which contractors are costing you the most money, or which clients are giving you the best ROI in seconds - all with the most recent data from Basecamp.

Project Visibility

Basecamp information is useful on its own, but to get the most out of the information you can combine it with data from other tools. Using the KPI tree tools, you can create goals to minimize new defects and maximize the number of people reporting defects. By adding data sources to the rest of the KPI tree, you can easily pinpoint when problems occur in your process and make efforts to improve them, wherever the data may reside.

How can I improve my sales performance?

Easy Insight gives you the reporting power you need to maximize your sales performance. Keep track of what's in your pipeline so that you don't lose track of crucial deals. Keep track of vital metrics such as average deal size, time to close, and win rates.

As soon as you connect your Easy Insight account, you'll have dashboards and reports providing views into your sales data, your leads, and your projects. You can export these reports into PDF and Excel, as well as setting them for recurring email delivery.

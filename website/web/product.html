What Is Easy Insight?

Business Intelligence On Demand

Traditional business intelligence has required you to make a large upfront investment--buy the software package, buy the hardware, plan out a data warehouse, design an ETL strategy, create report specifications, pass those specifications off to a report writer, and maybe start to see some results in the distant future. Easy Insight sets out to change that whole model by offering business intelligence through a Software as a Service model, with an overarching emphasis on ease of use and time to see a return on investment.

No More Infrastructure Worries

Taking advantage of the latest in cloud computing technology, Easy Insight offloads the complicated infrastructure required to power your reporting functionality, dramatically reducing the IT budget required to support reporting and analytics. We provide a secure, reliable, and scaleable environment for your data.

Ease of Use

Everything in Easy Insight has been designed from the approach of making the user experience as simple and effortless as possible. Before you can see any sort of valuable insights from your data, you have to get the data into the system--hence, we make it incredibly easy to get your data into the system. Whether your data is in spreadsheets, relational databaes, or any of a wide variety of other third party applications from Google Analytics to Salesforce.com, we enable you to get that data into Easy Insight as quickly as possible.

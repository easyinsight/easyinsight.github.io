iGoogle and Netvibes

You can easily add any Easy Insight report to your iGoogle or Netvibes dashboard pages. Want to assemble a dashboard of several reports? Just add and arrange them as you like within those existing dashboard applications, alongside any further widgets you may already have.

Desktop Display

You can easily view Easy Insight reports and KPI trees directly from your desktop through an Adobe AIR widget. This widget will work on any operating system that supports Adobe AIR, providing you with an easy way to just alt tab over and take a look at your business status.

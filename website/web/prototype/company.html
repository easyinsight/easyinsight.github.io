About Easy Insight

Easy Insight was founded in May of 2008 with a mission of creating the next generation of business intelligence. We aim to deliver software that meets the triple requirements of simplicity, affordability, and power. We go by the tagline of 'Visibility, Insight, and Improvement' because we see the problems we solve in terms of those three angles. For any problem, you need Visibility into the data. You need to be able to create Insights from that visibility. And finally, you need to be able to apply those Insights to drive continuous business Improvement.
